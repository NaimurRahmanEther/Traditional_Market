# User Guide: Authentication & Shopping Cart

## Getting Started

### First Time Users

1. **Create an Account**
   - Click the **Login icon** in the navbar (top right)
   - Click **"Sign up for free"** link
   - Fill in your details:
     - Full Name
     - Email
     - Phone Number
     - Password (min 6 characters)
     - Confirm Password
   - Check "I agree to the terms and conditions"
   - Click **"Create Account"**
   - You'll be logged in automatically and redirected to the homepage

2. **Or Login with Demo Account**
   - Click the **Login icon** in the navbar
   - Use these credentials:

## Shopping

### Browse Products

1. Navigate to **Products** or **Categories** from the menu
2. Click on any product to view details
3. See product images, price, description, and availability

### Add Items to Cart

1. On a product detail page:
   - Select the **quantity** using the + and - buttons
   - Click **"Add to Cart"**
   - You'll see a confirmation message

2. The **cart count** in the navbar updates automatically
   - Shows the total number of items
   - Displays as "9+" for 10+ items
   - Only shows when cart has items

### View & Manage Cart

1. Click the **shopping bag icon** in the navbar
2. In your cart, you can:
   - **Increase/Decrease quantities** using +/- buttons
   - **Remove items** with the trash icon
   - See **Subtotal, Shipping Cost, and Total**
3. Shipping is:
   - **Free** for orders over 5,000 BDT
   - **200 BDT** for orders under 5,000 BDT

### Continue Shopping

- Click **"Continue Shopping"** to go back to products
- Cart items are saved automatically

## Your Account

### Access Account Menu

1. Click your **user icon** in the navbar (top right)
2. You'll see a dropdown with:

**Admin Dashboard** (Admin only)

## Security

### Logging Out

1. Click your **user icon** in the navbar
2. Click **"Logout"**
3. You'll be logged out and returned to the homepage
4. Your cart is saved and will be restored when you log back in

### Keeping Your Account Safe

- **Never share** your login credentials
- **Use strong passwords** (at least 6 characters)
- **Log out** on shared computers
- Your cart data is stored **locally** on your device

---

## Cart Features

### Cart Persistence

- Your cart is **automatically saved** to your device
- Items stay in cart even if you:
  - Close the browser
  - Log out and log back in
  - Navigate away from the site
- Cart is stored per device (app can't access carts from other devices)

### Cart Operations

| Action | How |
|--------|-----|
| **Add to Cart** | Select quantity on product page + "Add to Cart" button |
| **Update Quantity** | Use +/- buttons in cart page |
| **Remove Item** | Click trash icon next to item in cart |
| **Clear Cart** | Remove all items individually |
| **View Cart** | Click shopping bag icon in navbar |

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Log In?
- Check your email and password spelling
- Ensure password is at least 6 characters
- Clear browser cache and try again
- Demo accounts work with any password >= 6 characters

### Cart Items Disappeared?
- Cart data is stored on your device
- If you delete browser data, cart will be cleared
- Always log in on the same device to see your cart
- Or try logging in with the same email on another device (browser will have a different cart)

### Can't Find My Order?
- Orders are shown in **"My Orders"**
- Only visible when logged in
- Limited to demo data (this is a demo site)

### Other Issues?
- Clear your browser cache
- Try a different browser
- Check your internet connection
- Contact support with error details
